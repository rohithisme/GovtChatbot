# Government Order (P) No. 52/2019/FIN

## Basic Information
- Date: May 3, 2019
- Location: Thiruvananthapuram
- S.R.O. No.: 382/2019
- Department: Finance (Rules-B) Department
- Type: Amendment to Kerala Service Rules

## Legal Authority
Exercising powers conferred by:
- Sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Kerala Public Services Act, 1968 (19 of 1968)
- Read with section 3 thereof

## Amendment Details
### Title and Commencement
1. Short Title: Kerala Service (Sixth Amendment) Rules, 2019
2. Effective Date: August 31, 2017

### Amendments to Part I of Kerala Service Rules
1. In sub-rule (ii) of rule 77:
   - Added "leave connected to inquiry into sexual harassment" after "child adoption leave"

2. After rule 102 B:
   - Added new "SECTION IX C - LEAVE CONNECTED TO INQUIRY INTO SEXUAL HARASSMENT"
   - Added new Rule 102 C with following provisions:

#### Rule 102 C Details
- Leave up to 90 days for aggrieved female employees during sexual harassment inquiry
- Conditions:
  a) Leave granted on recommendation of Internal/Local Complaints Committee
  b) Only during pendency of inquiry under Sexual Harassment Act, 2013
  c) Cannot be granted before inquiry start or after conclusion
  d) Can be combined with other leaves except LWA under Appendix XII A/XII B/XII C
  e) Not debited against leave account
  f) Granted on full pay

## Explanatory Note
### Government Order Reference
- G.O. (P) No. 119/2017/Fin. dated August 31, 2017
- Ordered special leave for sexual harassment inquiry cases
- Maximum 90 days during pendency of inquiry
- For aggrieved female employees of State Government

### Purpose
To give statutory validity to the above Government Order through amendments to Part I of Kerala Service Rules
