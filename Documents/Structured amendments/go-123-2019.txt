# Government Order (P) No. 123/2019/FIN

## Basic Information
- Date: September 6, 2019
- Location: Thiruvananthapuram
- S.R.O. No.: 640/2019
- Department: Finance (Pension-B) Department
- Type: Amendment to Kerala Service Rules

## Legal Authority
Exercising powers conferred by:
- Sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Kerala Public Services Act, 1968 (19 of 1968)
- Read with section 3 thereof

## Amendment Details
### Title and Commencement
1. Short Title: Kerala Service (Eighth Amendment) Rules, 2019
2. Effective Date: July 16, 2014

### Amendments to Part III of Kerala Service Rules
1. In Note 5 to rule 62:
   - Omitted the words "scale of"

2. In Note 2 to sub-rule (5) of rule 90:
   - Omitted the words "scale of"

## Explanatory Note
### Background
Existing provisions in Part III of Kerala Service Rules:
- Notional pay consequent on change in scale of pay due to General pay revision or otherwise is considered for:
  - Death-cum-Retirement Gratuity calculation
  - Family Pension calculation
- No provision for calculating these benefits based on change in pay in the same scale of pay

### Purpose
To rectify the above anomaly through suitable amendments to Part III of Kerala Service Rules
