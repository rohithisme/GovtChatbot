# Government Order (P) No. 20/2019/FIN

## Basic Information
- Date: February 22, 2019
- Location: Thiruvananthapuram
- S.R.O. No.: 153/2019
- Department: Finance (Pension-B) Department
- Type: Amendment to Kerala Service Rules

## Legal Authority
Exercising powers conferred by:
- Sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Kerala Public Services Act, 1968 (19 of 1968)
- Read with section 3 thereof

## Amendment Details
### Title and Commencement
1. Short Title: Kerala Service (Fourth Amendment) Rules, 2019
2. Effective Dates:
   - July 1, 2004: For sub-rule (1)
   - July 1, 2014: For sub-rule (2)

### Amendments to Part III, Rule 63
1. In 'Note 10':
   - Added new provisions regarding pension revision:
     - For those eligible for full pension: If revised pension is less than 50% of minimum revised scale pay, 50% shall be ensured
     - For those not eligible for full pension: Proportional amount based on qualifying service shall be ensured

2. Added new 'Note 10A':
   - Extended Note 10 benefits to employees with pre-revised pay in average emoluments due to LWA up to 4 months
   - Exception: Those who availed LWA under Appendix XII A, XII B and XII C

## Explanatory Note
### Government Orders Referenced
1. G.O. (P) No. 150/2015/Fin. dated April 24, 2015
   - Ordered minimum pension guarantee for those with mixed pre-revised and revised scale
   - Full pension eligible: Minimum 50% of revised scale minimum
   - Partial pension eligible: Proportional amount based on service length

2. G.O. (P) No. 278/2015/Fin. dated July 10, 2015
   - Clarified effective date as July 1, 2004 (8th Pay Revision date)

3. G.O. (P) No. 56/2018/Fin. dated April 3, 2018
   - Extended benefits to cases with LWA up to 4 months
   - Effective from July 1, 2014
   - Excluded LWA under Appendix XII A, XII B and XII C

### Purpose
To give statutory validity to the above Government Orders through amendments to Part III of Kerala Service Rules
