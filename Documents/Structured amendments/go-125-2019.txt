# Government Order (P) No. 125/2019/FIN

## Basic Information
- Date: September 6, 2019
- Location: Thiruvananthapuram
- S.R.O. No.: 644/2019
- Department: Finance (Pension-B) Department
- Type: Amendment to Kerala Service Rules

## Legal Authority
Exercising powers conferred by:
- Sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Kerala Public Services Act, 1968 (19 of 1968)
- Read with section 3 thereof

## Amendment Details
### Title and Commencement
1. Short Title: Kerala Service (Ninth Amendment) Rules, 2019
2. Effective Date: June 29, 2015

### Amendments to Part III of Kerala Service Rules
1. Rule 90:
   - In clause(vi) of sub-rule (13): Changed "Form 2" to "Form 2B"

2. Rule 110:
   - Changed "Form 11" to "Form 2A"

3. Rule 113:
   - In sub-clause (ii) of clause (b): Changed "Form 2" to "Form 2B"

4. Rule 115:
   - In clause (b): Changed "Form 2" to "Form 2B"
   - In clause (c):
     - Sub-clause (i): Changed "Form 2" to "Form 2B"
     - Sub-clause (ii): Changed "Form 2" to "Form 2B"

5. Rule 117:
   - In sub-rule (1): Changed "Form 2" to "Form 2B"

6. APPENDIX X, PART A, Rule 4:
   - Added: "If date of application for claim is within one year from the date of retirement, application for commutation of pension in Form 2A and 2B shall be furnished"

7. LIST OF FORMS:
   - Modified serial number 2:
     - 2A: "Formal application for Pension and Commutation"
     - 2B: "Formal application for Pension/Gratuity, Death-Cum Retirement Gratuity, Family Pension and Commutation of pension"
   - Omitted serial number 11 and its entry

8. Forms Section:
   - Replaced Form 2 with new Form 2A (Formal Application for Pension/Commutation)
   - Added new Form 2B (Comprehensive application form)
   - Omitted Form 11

### New Forms Details
#### Form 2A Contents
- Application details for pension and commutation
- Personal information
- Retirement details
- Commutation preferences
- Treasury details
- Declarations
- Required attachments

#### Form 2B Contents
- Comprehensive application for multiple benefits
- Personal and service details
- Pension calculation details
- Treasury information
- Family details
- Declarations
- Account enfacement details

## Explanatory Note
### Background
- Reference: G.O. (P) No.259/2015/Fin. dated June 29, 2015
- Decision to modify Form 2 and Form 11
- Integration of forms due to similar data requirements
- Modifications suggested by Principal Accountant General and public incorporated

### Purpose
To give statutory validity to the above Government Order through amendments to Part III of Kerala Service Rules
