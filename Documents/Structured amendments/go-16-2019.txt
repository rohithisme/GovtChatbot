# Government Order (P) No. 16/2019/FIN

## Basic Information
- Date: February 20, 2019
- Location: Thiruvananthapuram
- S.R.O. No.: 150/2019
- Department: Finance (Pension-B) Department
- Type: Amendment to Kerala Service Rules

## Legal Authority
Exercising powers conferred by:
- Sub-section (1) of section 2 of the Kerala Public Services Act, 1968 (19 of 1968)
- Read with section 3 thereof

## Amendment Details
### Title and Commencement
1. Short Title: Kerala Service (First Amendment) Rules, 2019
2. Effective Date: September 1, 2014

### Amendments to Part III of Kerala Service Rules
1. In sub-rule (9) of rule 90:
   - Added "or grand parent" after "natural guardian"

2. In Note 2 below rule 118:
   - Replaced "his/her guardian" with "his/her de-facto guardian"
   - Added "or grand parent" after "natural guardian"

## Explanatory Note
### Background
- Previous conditions for payment of family pension and Death-cum-Retirement Gratuity/share to minor children through de-facto guardian caused delays
- Resulted in hardship to eligible minor children of deceased Government employees

### Government Order Reference
- G.O. (P) No. 381/2014/Fin., dated September 1, 2014
- Ordered that benefits due to minor children shall be payable to their grandparent
- Required production of de-facto guardianship certificate from District Collector or Tahsildar

### Purpose
To give statutory validity to the above Government Order through amendments to Part III of Kerala Service Rules
