RULES  
    1. Short title and commencement.-(}) These rules may be called . .  th~ Kerala Service (Fourth Amendment) Rules, 2019.  
(2) In rule 2,- 
         ( a) sub-rule (I) shall be deemed to have come into force on  the 1st day of July, 2004;  
         (b) sub-rule (2) shall be deemed to have come into force on  the 1st day of July, 2014.  
    2. Amendment of the Rules.-In Part III of the Kerala Service  Rules, in rule 63,- 
      (1) in 'Note 10', the following sentences shall be added at the  end, namely:- 
        "While revising pension for those who have the eligibility for full  pension, if the pension so arrived at is less than fifty percent of the  minimum of the revised scale of pay of the post from which he/she  retired; fifty percent of the minimum of the revised scale of pay shall be  ensured. For those, who are -not eligible for full pension, the proportion of  full pension based on the length of qualifying service shall be ensured.";  
(2) after 'Note 10', the following note shall be inserted, namely:- 
           "Note JOA.-The benefit contemplated as per 'Note 10' above  -~ shall be extended to those employees in respect of whom pre-revised pay  forms part of average emoluments consequent on availing Leave Without  Allowance upto four months during the last ten months of service except  those who avail Leave Without Allowance under Appendix XII A, XII B  and XII C".
